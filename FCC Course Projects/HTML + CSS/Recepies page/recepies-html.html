1 Chorizo Pasta

Ingredients

  • Passata
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Spaghetti
  • Chorizo, diced
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a separate pan, heat oil and add onion. Cook until soft.
  4. Add chorizo and cook until browned.
  5. Add passata, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Drain spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  7. Serve with grated parmesan on top.
