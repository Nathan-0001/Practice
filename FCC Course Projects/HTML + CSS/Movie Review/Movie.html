La La Land

La La Land is a 2016 American musical romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Damien Chazelle. The film stars Ryan Gosling as a jazz musician and Emma Stone as an aspiring actress, who meet and fall in love while pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles.
