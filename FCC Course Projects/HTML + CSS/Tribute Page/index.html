Son Heung-min

Son Heung-Min is a footballer for the club Tottenham Hotspur.

Son Heung-Min Has played for Tottenham for 10 years and has always given his all for the club and at the end of the 2025 season his work finally payed off with him being able to lift the clubs first european trophy since 1972 as the captain.
